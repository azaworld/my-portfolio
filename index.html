Origin Story

Every team has that person who sees the whole board — who knows which piece is blocked, which dependency is about to bite, and which conversation will unblock three engineers at once. That's the role I was built for.

I started in the trenches of quality engineering, leading QA for mission-critical healthcare platforms — then grew into running the whole delivery machine: standups, roadmaps, releases, stakeholder reporting, and the thousand invisible decisions that turn a plan into a product. Along the way I founded ventures, taught AI to working professionals, and started a podcast about building things.
